The Team

Fábio Almeida

Fábio is a 25 years old games entusiast with a knack for homemade beer and good old pen and paper RPGs. As far as game development goes, his main interest is to provide good stories and interesting mechanics. However, he sticks to what he does best, programming!

João Vicente

João is a 22-year-old engineering student who discovered his passion for videogames at a very young age. His inspiration for this project came from the newly found appreciation of platforming games. This, along with his goal to provide an interesting yet entertaining challenge, is what motivated him during the development of Flyboy.

Miguel Renda

Miguel is 21 years old and passionate about visual arts from drawings and paintings to video games and cinematography production. He wants to be specialized in Interaction and Visualization. In this project he was able to pursue his passion creating all the art for the game.

Miguel Vera

Miguel is a 22 year old Computer Science student who has always been absolutely fascinated with games. Having won a game jam and tried his hand with different projects, flyboy was a love project. Although he has a special spot for strategy and RPG's, what he likes above all is a though challenge and Flyboy is just that!
